What is NiranX Universe?

NiranX Universe is a comprehensive web platform combining artificial intelligence, study planning, collaboration, and creative tools in one place. Students, teachers, and self-learners use NiranX to plan their week, master topics with spaced repetition, generate quizzes and notes from any document, and join real-time classrooms.

Core features

  • AI Corner — chat with 500+ frontier AI models in one hub.
  • XGenesis AI — premium AI gateway for advanced reasoning.
  • Smart Timetable — adaptive weekly schedules.
  • Flashcards — spaced repetition decks with AI generation.
  • AI Quiz Generator — instant quizzes from any source.
  • Document Scanner — OCR and AI summarization.
  • Pomodoro Timer — focus sessions with analytics.
  • Live Classroom — real-time video, screen share, breakout rooms.
  • Xvibe Music — study-friendly streaming and playlists.
  • Cornell Notes — structured note-taking.
  • Study Rooms — collaborative focus spaces.
  • Mind Maps — visual knowledge graphs.

Frequently asked questions

Is NiranX Universe free?

Yes — the core platform and AI tools are free for students.

Which AI models are included?

NiranX integrates 500+ frontier models including GPT-5, Gemini 2.5/3, DeepSeek, Groq, OpenRouter, and more via XGenesis AI.

Does it work offline?

Yes. NiranX is a PWA — installable on desktop and mobile with offline caching for key pages.
